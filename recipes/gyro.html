Gyro

Description

An eastern mediterranean recipe from the areas of Turkey and Greece, the Gyro is doner kebab meat sliced and served in a wrapped pita bread with other ingredients, such as tomato, onion, and tzatziki sauce

Ingredients

  • Pita Bread
  • Kebab Meat
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Tzatziki Sauce
  • Feta Cheese

Steps

  1. Lay pita flat
  2. Add desired Kebab meat
  3. Add sliced tomato and onion
  4. Top tzatziki sauce and feta cheese/li>
  5. roll pita
