Sandwich

Description

A recipe consisting of two pieces of bread with meat, cheese, or other filling between them, eaten as a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Turkey
  • Ham
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Apply mayonaise and mustard to bread
  2. Lay down desired amount of Turkey, Ham, and Bacon on the bottom piece of bread
  3. Slice Tomato and Lettuce
  4. Top meat with desired amount of Lettuce and Tomato
  5. Lay top piece of bread to finish the recipe
